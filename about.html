About

Hey, I’m Natalie! I am a DevOps Engineer based in Austin, TX, and a lifelong learner. Over six years ago, I fell in love with web development and the excitement of cutting-edge technology. Since then, I’ve made it my goal to solve complex problems using a combination of business acumen and technical know-how.

My enthusiasm and drive for innovation stem from my unique strengths in envisioning the big picture and translating technical requirements into effective solutions for enterprise-level implementations. Additionally, I excel at working collaboratively across various teams to enhance content quality and identify any gaps in documentation that may affect its accessibility to users.

I am a natural problem solver fascinated by programming, platform modernization, and eliminating manual work by writing software.

The web is incredible. It allows you to showcase your established business, market yourself, sell products and services, and reach a global audience. I believe in the world we live in today, your website and the technology you use are some of the most important assets you can have as a business owner.

My expertise lies in developing both impactful content and efficient systems that optimize developer operations. I'm passionate about collaborating with teams to devise and execute projects that are infused with creativity, strategic thinking, and a distinctive flair.

I possess a strong sense of conscientiousness and excel at working independently in dynamic and self-driven agile environments. When I'm not immersed in my work, I'm likely exploring new culinary horizons in the kitchen, pushing my limits at a hot pilates class, or embarking on exciting adventures to discover new cities with my closest companion.
